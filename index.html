Explore Oxford

Hello Explorers - Welcome to Oxford! Oxford has so much to offer to every kind of visitor - whether you are looking for things to do, somewhere to eat or drink, or a place to rest your head at the end of a busy day, Explore Oxford can help.

Oxford University is made up of 38 independent colleges, all listed here. Their buildings can be found all over the city. These are open to visitors at specific times, a visit to Oxford is not complete without stopping off inside some of our beautiful colleges! Oxford is home to world-class museums, including the Ashmolean Museum and other university museums such as Pitt Rivers and the Bodleian Libraries.

We are spoilt with lots of green space in Oxford, all within walking distance of the bustling historic centre. Feeling peckish? Thirsty? Need a place to stay? We hope you enjoy using this map to plan your whole visit and enrich your Oxford experience.
